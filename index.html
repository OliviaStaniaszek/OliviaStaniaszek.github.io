Interlocking Wooden Dragon

Jan 2022 - May 2022
Brief

Design a plain object which uses an interlocking mechanism,Made out of wood, less than 300mm x 150mm x 150mm

Response

A carved dragon head made out of wood in two pieces, a skull and a head. Inspired by slide release buckles. Designed to be satisfying to play with and hold

Paper Water Lily Column

Sept 2021 - Oct 2022
Brief

Make a three dimensional object, an artefact more like a piece of art, a personal response, expressing the idea of a column

Response

A column made entirely out of paper and cardboard made to look like a water lily and lily pads. Inspired by Ancient Egyptian columns and the lily pads in my pond at home

Carpeggia - Lower body rain protection

Oct 2022 - Dec 2022
Brief

Design an artefact to protect from wind and/or rain

Response

A lower body garment made from old raincoats. Inspired by Japanese Samurai Armour. Aim to allow the user to maintain their stylish outfit, without having to cover it with a long raincoat.
